# India Power Capacity & Energy Mix Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes India’s power generation capacity with a focus on:

- Total installed capacity growth over time

- Renewable vs fossil fuel energy mix

- State-wise capacity distribution and renewable penetration

The dashboards are designed to support both executive-level overview and state-level analytical insights.

## Dashboards Included
### National Overview Dashboard

**Purpose:** High-level view of India’s power sector

**Key Insights:**

- Total installed capacity trend (2019–2024)

- Fossil vs Renewable capacity comparison

- Renewable share (%) of total capacity

- Interactive filters by Year and State

### State-wise Energy Analysis Dashboard

**Purpose:** Drill-down analysis at state level

**Key Insights:**

- Top states by total installed capacity

- Fossil vs Renewable capacity by state

- Renewable share (%) across states

- State-level KPIs with interactive slicer

### Key Metrics

- Total Installed Capacity (MW)

- Renewable Capacity (MW)

- Fossil Capacity (MW)

- Renewable Share (%)

### Tools & Technologies
